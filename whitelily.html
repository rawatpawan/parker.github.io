The Location Advantage

The Dream Location

The luxurious White Lily enjoys a bouquet of advantages of being located near the NH-1 and KMP Expressway.

To top it all, it has proximity to one of the fastest developing zones in the region, with the upcoming Rapid Rail Transit System and many mega-crore projects sure to turn Sonepat into a most sought after destination.

While the RRTS will reduce travel time from Sonepat to Kashmiri Gate, Delhi by 20 minutes, people will be able to reach the international airport in 45 minutes.

White Lily is also conveniently located near to Rajiv Gandhi Education City which is spread over 5000 acre of land and this education city is unfurling a new era in education under one campus.
